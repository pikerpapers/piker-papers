A short clip circulated online under the title *Hasan Piker Singing Bella ciao*, with an accompanying X post asserting that Piker opened his stream by singing or humming *Bella Ciao*. The clip was shared after public reporting on Charlie Kirk's killing and claims that one of the bullets used by the attacker had been etched with the words "Bella Ciao."

This file documents the timing and circulation of that clip rather than treating it as an isolated moment. In context, critics pointed to the choice of song as notable because *Bella Ciao* had already become part of the public discussion surrounding the killing. The entry preserves the clip and post as source material for review.
