This file documents a clip from Hasan Piker's stream in which he speaks approvingly about Mao Zedong and explicitly says he would never make fun of him. The linked Streamable source is preserved here as the primary record of the remark.

At the same time, Piker qualifies the praise by adding that there were "a lot of fucking excesses." The entry is intended as a source-backed archive record of the clip itself, preserving the language and source location for review rather than relying on paraphrase or secondhand description alone.

**Direct quote**

> "Who thought it was like making fun of Mao Zedong, which I never would and never will. Mao Zedong is one of the great leaders of this world, okay? A man who changed the entire universe, the entire planet, okay? ... I mean, there was a lot of fucking excesses, let's be real."
